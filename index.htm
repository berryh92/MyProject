Welcome to Raving Researchers!

- -

We are a small, independent research sharing group started by Hayley Berry, our current main site administrator.

This website was first created as a means of effective communication between students, researchers and supervisors alike.

From taking our first steps into the technical world of online research sharing, we are hoping for much bigger plans to come together for us in the future.

One of our most ultimate aims is for this site to become one of the most popular means of research sharing across the Web, helping many people come together and help each other out with ideas, informative papers and much more.

Work topics submitted here at Raving Researchers stem from a range of subject areas such as the Sciences, Literature and Computing, so we're confident you will be able to find what you need.

Our site is 100% dedicated to getting your papers the frontpage publicity they deserve!

Your research is as big a part of your work as it is ours and it is because of this, that we pride ourselves on such high dedication to finding you the most accurate, up-to-date information.

Learn a little more about us and the way we operate on the 'About Us' page above - if you like what you see, don't hesitate to sign-up and get submitting your papers!
